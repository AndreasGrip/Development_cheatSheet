### Multi select
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>D</kbd> select the next occurrence  
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd> + <kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>D</kbd> skip the next occurrence  
<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>U</kbd> undo one of the above
